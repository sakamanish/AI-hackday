folk dance

Bhangra

lively, energetic dance form originating from the state of Punjab, usually performed during harvest festivals like Baisakhi. It involves rhythmic movements, vigorous footwork, and a lot of jumps.

Garba

A traditional dance performed during the festival of Navratri, Garba involves circular movements with clapping and rhythmic steps. It is often performed around a statue of Goddess Durga.

Dandiya

Similar to Garba but involves the use of wooden sticks, called dandiya, which dancers strike together in rhythm while moving in circles. It is also performed during Navratri
